What is ?

Upchanges is a social media that connects you stronger with people who you knew about but are not closed with, with people who share the same interest through providing a place where you can create and do meaningful actions with them.

We also provide a database full of problems, projects and ideas in the world for change makers and students to use and build upon on. 　

Upchanges is completely free and has no advertisements so please support us by clicking the link below and donate!
